"I never regret making a movie, but if i could, i would eliminate Titanic from my filmography"

FILMAX has an exclusive sneak peak of the chat, in which our interviewer, questioned DiCaprio about what he wants to accomplish before turning 50 next year.

"You know what? Just one more movie," said DiCaprio, hinting that he hopes to get back in front of the camera for another role. "One more movie."

Prior to the question, interviewer and DiCaprio reflected on how quickly the years have gone by. "You're 49 years old. How the hell did that happen?" teased de interviewer, who had often interviewed DiCaprio during his 13-year reign covering movies for MTV News.

"I have no idea," DiCaprio said in response, recalling, "We were talking about our first interview, that may have been 30 years ago."
